Principal Investigator

Dr. Satish K. Panda

Assistant Professor at IIT Bhubaneswar (School of Mechanical Sciences) and affiliated with the AI and HPC Research Center. Previously an Assistant Professor at IIT Guwahati and Research Fellow at Singapore Eye Research Institute.

Dr. Panda earned his PhD in Biomedical Engineering from the National University of Singapore. His research centers on image processing and the development of AI and deep learning networks for healthcare and computer vision applications.

PhD (2015-2019) National University of Singapore
MTech (2011-2013) IIT Delhi, India

Research Areas & Projects

Our multidisciplinary team tackles complex challenges in Healthcare and Underwater exploration.

Schizophrenia Diagnostics (ICMR)

Grant: 95 Lakh

AI-powered tool for autonomous identification of retinal phenotypes as potential diagnostic biomarkers for Schizophrenia spectrum disorders.

Glaucoma Detection (SERB-SRG)

Grant: 32 Lakh

Fully automated diagnostic software for evidence-based glaucoma detection by identifying structural biomarkers of the optic nerve head.

Underwater Monitoring (IITG TIH)

Grant: 45 Lakh

An intelligent vision system for autonomous health monitoring of underwater oil pipelines.

Cranial Implants & VR (VARCOE)
